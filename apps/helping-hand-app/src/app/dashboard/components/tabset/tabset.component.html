Welcome to Helping Hand

Helping Hand helps you get that little bit of help when you need it and gives you a chance to help others out as well.

Important: by default Helping Hand shares your email with other memebers of the community so that they're able to respond to your favour requests. We recommend that you go to the profile tab and customize how you prefer for other users to contact you e.g provide your instagram username instead.
